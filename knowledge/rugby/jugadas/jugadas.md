## Short or Frontal Plays

### Bulita
Play that creates a 3 vs 2 in midfield with the fly-half, inside center and blindside wing.
The fly-half fixes their opponent, the inside center seeks a short angle to the inside shoulder.
The blindside wing hides behind the fly-half until the last moment.

### Bandeja/Maca/Pomelo
The fly-half cuts toward the inside shoulder of the opposing inside center.
The inside center crosses and the outside center comes on a short angle simultaneously.
- Maca: blindside wing crosses after the inside center
- Pomelo: blindside wing loops behind the outside center

### Adelante
The fly-half aims at the opposing inside center, the inside center mimics their run toward the opposing outside center,
and the outside center crosses behind. The blindside wing goes toward the openside behind the inside center.

### Boca
Always executed from left to right. The centers run straight lines toward the defense
and find space at the last moment. The blindside wing loops behind as a trailer but not too deep.

### Bufetero (short/long)
Centers cross without the ball. The outside center comes short in front and the inside center
goes long behind. The blindside wing supports inside.

## Long Plays

### Quilombo
The fly-half passes to the inside center who attacks forward supported by the outside center.
The fly-half loops behind with support from the blindside wing, fullback and openside wing.

### Francia
Executed from left to right. The scrum-half engages the opposing fly-half and passes to the inside center.
The fly-half loops behind the center with multiple passing options.

### Lanzini
ALWAYS from right to left. The blindside wing receives directly from the scrum-half and aims for the inside shoulder
of the opposing 12. Creates a 4 vs 3 if played behind.

## Midfield Plays

### Chiefs
Fly-half and fullback behind the scrum move right when the ball comes out.
The scrum-half attacks and passes to the inside center (12) who drives inward to fix.

### Waikato
Fly-half and fullback move left. A bufetero (cross) is executed between the outside center
and the fullback with two passing options.
